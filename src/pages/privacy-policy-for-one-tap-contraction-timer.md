## Information We Collect

### 1. **Information You Provide**

- **Contraction Records**: Start time, end time, duration, and frequency of contractions
- **User Settings**: Preferences such as haptic feedback on/off
- **PDF Exports**: When you choose to export your contraction history

**Storage:** All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage. We do NOT store your contraction data on our servers.

### 2. **Information Collected Automatically**

#### **Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)**

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:

- Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- IP address and approximate location
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
- Usage data and app interactions

#### **In-App Purchase Data (Google Play Billing)**

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features:

- **Premium PDF Export**: Removes watermark and unlocks unlimited exports
- **Ad-Free Experience**: Removes all advertisements

**Purchase Information Collected:**

- Purchase transaction details (handled by Google Play)
- Purchase status (premium/free) stored locally on your device
- No payment information is stored in the App

**Third-Party Privacy Policy:** [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

#### **Analytics Data** (Optional - if you add analytics later)

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with the App, including:

- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage (e.g., button presses, PDF exports)
- Crash reports and performance data
