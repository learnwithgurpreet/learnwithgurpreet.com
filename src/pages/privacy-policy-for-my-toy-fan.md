## 1. Introduction

"My Toy Fan - Kids Fun & Sleep" ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. We have designed this app to be a safe, premium, and ad-free environment compliant with the Google Play "Designed for Families" program.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you or your child uses our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

We take your privacy seriously. We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal information.

### Personal Information

We do not ask for or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.

### Non-Personal Information

The app operates locally on your device. We do not transmit usage data, analytics, or crash reports to external servers. Any settings (like volume or fan speed) are stored locally on the device to improve the user experience and are not shared.

## 3. No Advertising

This is a Paid Application (Premium).

- No Ads: We do not display any third-party advertisements.

- No Tracking: We do not use any advertising SDKs (Software Development Kits) or tracking cookies.

- No In-App Purchases: The app is a one-time purchase with no hidden fees or locked content.

## 4. Permissions

To ensure the safety and privacy of children, our app requires minimal permissions:

- No Internet Required: The app functions entirely offline. We do not request internet access permissions for data transmission or advertising.

## 5. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance)

We strictly comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

- Zero Data Collection: We do not collect any personal data from children.

- Safe Environment: There are no links to social media, no external web links, and no interactive features that allow children to communicate with others.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
